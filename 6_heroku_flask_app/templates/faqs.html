FAQs

According to the Children's Bureau, ACEs "are traumatic events occuring before the age of 18."

It is usually broken down into three categories: Abuse, Neglect and Household Challenges.

The data was collected from the CDC's Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) ACE module for the years between 2009 - 2012.

The data collected only included questions for these two categories. Neglect items were not added to the BRFSS ACE module until 2019.

It was one of the first studies looking at the effects of ACE on later-life and wellbeing.
